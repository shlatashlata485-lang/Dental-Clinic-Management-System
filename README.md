# 🦷 Dental Clinic Management System (End-to-End Solution)
### Built & Designed by **SHLATA HUB** (Mohamed Shlata)

A comprehensive, offline-first, and highly intuitive desktop management system designed specifically for dental clinics. This project is built from scratch (End-to-End) to digitalize daily clinic workflows, manage patient records, and provide real-time business and clinical analytics.

---

## 📸 System Overview

### 1. Central Control Panel (Main Hub)
A clean grid-based navigation system to access all clinic operations (Control Panel, Reservations, Patient Records, Digital Dental Chart, Invoices, and Inventory).

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Main Hub](Screenshot%20(126).png)

### 2. Business Analytics Dashboard
Real-time monitoring of monthly profits, active cases, and urgent alerts (e.g., debt accumulation warnings and interactive calendar filters).

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Dashboard](Screenshot%20(127).png)

### 3. Patient Records & Medical Files
Comprehensive database with advanced search filters (by Name or Phone Number). Track active chronic illnesses, age, visit frequency, and complete historical diagnosis records. One-click **Export to Excel** feature for medical reporting and data backups.

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Patient Records](Screenshot%20(128).png)

### 4. Interactive Dental Anatomy Chart (FDI System)
Dynamic, clickable graphical map of the complete dental arch (32 teeth). Color-coded clinical status indicators for each tooth: **Decay (Red), Filling (Teal), Extraction (Dark Grey), Root Canal (Yellow), Implant (Blue), Surgery (Brown), Braces (Pink), Bleaching (Cyan), and Healthy (Light Blue).** Instantly updates individual patient medical files with active dental states.

> Includes **Smart Clinical Assistant (AI-Powered):** Integrated instant diagnostic assistant. Doctors can type symptoms (e.g., severe localized pain, gum swelling) to receive immediate technical support or clinical suggestions.

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic FDI Tooth Chart & AI Assistant](Screenshot%20(129).png)

### 5. Financial & Debt Management System
Transparent ledger showing overall invoices, total dues, total paid amounts, and outstanding debts. Dedicated installment tracker for patients undergoing long-term procedures (e.g., orthodontics or implants).

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Financial Ledger](Screenshot%20(130).png)

### 6. Inventory & Medical Supplies Store
Comprehensive warehouse system tracking medical tools and clinic materials (like Anesthetics, Composites, etc.). Automated alerts for low stock levels based on a custom threshold (Warning Level).

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Inventory Management](Screenshot%20(131).png)

### 7. Integrated Booking & WhatsApp Reminders
Modern reservation desk tracking daily appointments, booking statuses (e.g., regular visit vs. follow-up), and amounts paid. **Instant WhatsApp Reminder Button** to directly message scheduled patients, reducing clinic no-shows.

![SHLATA HUB Dental Clinic Reservations & WhatsApp Integration](Screenshot%20(132).png)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

* **Frontend UI:** React, Tailwind CSS, HTML5, CSS3
* **Charts & Analytics:** Chart.js / custom SVG mapping for FDI Dental Chart
* **Database & Offline Capabilities:** IndexedDB (for high-performance offline data integrity and instant sync)
* **Branding & System Integrator:** **SHLATA HUB**
